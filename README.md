## 📚 Experiments Overview

### 🗺️ Gneiss Rides – Geology Meets Trail Maps
**What it does:** Overlays geological maps on hiking trails so you can see what rock formations you're walking through.

**Who it's for:** Geology students, curious hikers, outdoor educators.

**Try it:** [Launch the app →](https://nsuurmey.github.io/gneiss-rides/)

---

### 💰 Block Party – Predicting Oil & Gas Lease Bids
**What it does:** Uses machine learning to find patterns in oil/gas lease bidding behavior.

**The insight:** By analyzing historical bid placement, block relinquishment, and operator behavior, can we predict future bids?

**Who it's for:** Energy sector analysts, data scientists, geospatial researchers.

**Explore:** [Jupyter notebooks →](https://github.com/nsuurmey/block-party)

## 💡 Glossary: Key Terms

| Term | What It Means | Why It Matters |
|------|---------------|----------------|
| **Gneiss** | A common metamorphic rock (pronounced "nice") | The pun in the name—"Gneiss Rides" = nice trails with geologic context |
| **Block (oil/gas)** | A rectangular lease area where companies bid to drill | Understanding bid patterns helps predict energy sector activity |
| **Socratic Questioning** | A method of teaching through questions rather than answers | Builds deeper understanding; useful for brainstorming & problem-solving |
| **Steel Man Argument** | Arguing against the *strongest* version of your opponent's idea | Better than a straw man—finds real flaws, not fake ones |
